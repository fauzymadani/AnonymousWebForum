Forum Rules

Welcome to our anonymous forum! To ensure a respectful and enjoyable experience for everyone, please follow these simple rules:

  1. Respect others. Harassment, bullying, or attacking other users is not allowed. Treat everyone with kindness and respect.
  2. No illegal content. Posting or linking to illegal content (such as pirated materials or harmful content) is strictly prohibited.
  3. No spamming. Do not post spam or advertisements. This includes posting repetitive content or links to promote something.
  4. No personal information. To protect your privacy and anonymity, do not share personal information (yours or others'). This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, etc.
  5. Report violations. If you see any rule violations or inappropriate content, please report it to the moderators.
  6. Follow local laws. While our forum allows anonymous posting, you are still expected to follow the laws of your country.
  7. Username generation. Your username will be automatically generated for anonymity. Do not attempt to impersonate others or bypass this system.
  8. Moderation decisions are final. Moderators reserve the right to remove any posts or ban users at their discretion to maintain a healthy environment.
  9. English only. To maintain clarity, all posts must be in English unless specified otherwise by a category.

By participating in this forum, you agree to these rules. Enjoy your stay and happy posting!
